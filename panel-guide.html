Panel Guide

OpsLink Hosting uses Pterodactyl — a powerful game and bot hosting control panel.

Accessing the Panel

  • Go to panel.opslinkcad.com.
  • Log in with your credentials from your order email.

Using SFTP

Access files using an FTP client like FileZilla:

  • Host: panel.opslinkcad.com
  • Port: 2022
  • Username: Your panel username

Console & Scheduling

The console allows you to manage your server directly and schedule automatic restarts or updates.

Resource Monitoring

You can view CPU, RAM, and disk usage per server in real time from your dashboard.
