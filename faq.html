Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to the most common questions about OpsLink Hosting, your control panel, and service management.

How do I access my server?

All servers are managed through our control panel at panel.opslinkcad.com. Your login credentials are emailed to you after your server is created. Once logged in, you can start, stop, or configure your server instantly.

Can I create servers myself?

No. All servers are provisioned manually by OpsLink staff to ensure proper setup and resource allocation. Once provisioned, you have full control through your panel.

How do I connect via SFTP?

Use an SFTP client such as FileZilla or WinSCP with the following details:

  • Host: panel.opslinkcad.com
  • Port: 2022
  • Username: your panel username
  • Password: your panel password

SFTP access allows you to upload, download, and edit server files directly from your computer.

Where are servers hosted?

OpsLink currently operates in three main regions:

  • NA-East: Ideal for US and Canadian communities
  • NA-Central: Balanced for nationwide latency
  • EU: For European player bases

Additional regions may be added as demand grows.

Depending on your plan, automatic backups occur daily or weekly. You can also create manual backups from your panel anytime. Backups are stored on secure NVMe-based storage and can be restored instantly.
